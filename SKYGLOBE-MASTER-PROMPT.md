# SKYGLOBE GROUP — THE MASTER WEB & ECOSYSTEM PROMPT
### The complete, self-contained brief for designing and building the ENTIRE SkyGlobe Group ecosystem — every page, portal, platform and flow. Hand this to any designer, developer, or AI and nothing else is needed.

# PART A — WHO THIS IS

**SKYGLOBE GROUP** is a global digital ecosystem headquartered in Africa — positioned among the
defining platforms of the century. Motto, everywhere, always: **ONE WORLD. ONE MISSION. ✦**

It is NOT a travel website. Travel is one service the Group renders; it does not define the Group.

**The family:**

| Entity | Role | Identity |
|---|---|---|
| **SKYGLOBE GROUP** | Global parent — innovation & infrastructure | Deep Royal Blue + Gold, globe emblem with the orange NORIA ✦ at upper-right |
| **NORIA ✦** | The intelligence layer — AI inside every service (never “a chatbot”) | Orange ✦ + gold |
| **TERRA** | Africa's trust infrastructure — identity, verification, records, security. Africa-only. | Green `#3fae6a` + deep blue; globe-and-orbit logo with orange star |
| **YUNEX** | Africa's economic infrastructure — payments, commerce, cross-border trade. Africa-only. | Blue→purple gradient `#3d5af1→#b326d9` + gold; Y logo with orange star |
| **Service verticals** | Global Mobility 🌐 · SkyGlobe Academy 🎓 · Legal & Trust Services 📜 · Digital Identity 🪪 · Finance & Payments 💳 · Innovation & Technology 🚀 | Each carries the parent brand |

**Dependency creed:** *Terra verifies. YUNEX moves. NORIA thinks.*
**YUNEX product family:** YUNEX Pay · **YUNEX Trade** (flagship — “Africa's gateway to global
commerce”; corridors are features, never brands: China Corridor first, then Gulf, Europe, and
the Africa Corridor) · YUNEX Market. **Trade rule:** real national-identity KYC via TERRA for
every person; government business registration + legal identity of every owner and partner for
businesses; end-to-end encryption; real-time trade between verified humans; zero tolerance for
illegal activity. *“No verification, no trade.”*

**Voice:** confident, warm, precise, claims-honest — never “first in the world”; always
“a new architecture uniquely designed for Africa's needs — African-owned, African-governed.”
The emotional target: *“I am not visiting a website. I am entering an ecosystem.”* — the trust
of a financial institution, the innovation of a tech company, the elegance of a luxury brand,
the accessibility of a consumer platform.

# PART B — THE DESIGN SYSTEM (LAW)

## B1. Surfaces — light-first, sovereign
White and soft-white dominate (~60%). Dark is deliberate, rare, SHORT — never long dark walls.

| Surface | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Public heroes | Luminous white (`#eef4ff → #f8fafc` radial) with faint gold/blue glows and a subtle dot grid |
| Content, forms, docs | White `#ffffff` on soft white `#F8FAFC`; feature strips `#eef2f8` |
| CTA bands | Deep Royal Blue `#0A2E65 → #15418c` — short bands only |
| Footer | Midnight Navy `#041022` |
| **CEO & staff portals** | Deep midnight command-center (dark by design: gold data glows, eyes rest) |

## B2. Color law
- **Deep Royal Blue `#0A2E65`** — structure, navigation, trust (~25%)
- **Premium Gold `#D4A73A`** (text-on-light: `#a87016`) — primary CTAs, highlights, key metrics (~10%, scarce by law)
- **Vibrant Orange `#e65100→#ff9f1c`** — RESERVED: the NORIA ✦ + live activity only; never errors (~5%)
- **Text on light:** pure black `#000` for hero headlines, `#111827/#1f2937` primary, `#374151` secondary, `#6B7280` supporting
- **Text on dark:** white primary, `#D1D5DB` secondary, `#9CA3AF` muted
- Platform accents are constitutional (see Part A table) — NEVER give YUNEX orange
- Semantic: success green, warning amber, danger red — always icon + word, never color alone

## B3. Type, shape, motion
**Inter** everywhere (system fallback); Georgia serif only for display/manifesto headlines.
8-point spacing. Radii: buttons/inputs 12px, cards 14–16px, dialogs 18–20px. Shadows soft
(`0 6px 20px rgba(4,16,34,.06)`). Motion: fade/slide/gentle scale/soft glow; nothing instant,
nothing decorative; `prefers-reduced-motion` honored. Microcopy short and imperative
(“Continue”, “Verify Identity”).

## B4. Signature components
- **Premium card:** white, hairline border, colored TOP border in the owning platform's accent, gentle hover lift, one primary action
- **Kicker chip:** uppercase letter-spaced deep-gold label on `#fdf6e3` pill crowning each section
- **Buttons:** primary gold-filled (dark text) · secondary blue-filled (white text) · tertiary outlined white (charcoal text) · text-only minor; 48px targets
- **Manifesto block:** white card, 4px accent left border, serif prose
- **The NORIA star ✦:** upper-right of every logo, small, orange, permanent — “intelligence lives inside everything we build”

## B5. Navigation
- **Desktop (white glass bar):** Logo✦ · Home · **Platforms ✦** (featured dropdown: NORIA/TERRA/YUNEX each with icon, name, identity line — the platforms hold court with authority) · Global Mobility ▾ (ALL travel: visas, permits, recruitment, flights, hotels, insurance, packages, events, destinations) · Academy ▾ · Solutions ▾ · Company ▾ · Login · gold CTA. No search bar in the nav.
- **Mobile:** fixed WHITE bottom bar — Home · Discover · **NORIA ✦ elevated gold circle at center** · Services · Profile — plus full-screen menu whose FIRST section is Platforms ✦.
- **Every page** carries floating Back ‹ › Next navigation. Wizards always have Next/Back.

# PART D — THE FLOWS (UX behavior contracts)

1. **Contact & department messaging:** any listed email opens an ON-SITE form (name, email,
   service selector for that division, message) → AI Reception triages instantly → client
   gets an on-screen confirmation + email receipt echoing their message → AI auto-answers
   when confident, from that division's real address → humans take over when flagged.
   Explicit department choice is AUTHORITATIVE — AI may reclassify only General messages.
   CEO's address is never listed publicly, never auto-answered; public submissions to it
   silently reroute to General.
2. **Zero-silent-failure:** any failed automated action (email, delivery, payment fulfilment)
   surfaces to the human queue and/or error log. Email has automatic failover
   (Resend → Brevo) plus portal-first delivery (account holders get every AI/staff reply in
   their in-app inbox — zero email dependence).
3. **Payments:** initialize → verify → fulfil, one engine for all verticals; instant products
   deliver signed, expiring unlock links; the CEO can issue ANY paid service free
   (complimentary grants with claim links that skip payment).
4. **Applications:** reference numbers (SKY-YYYY-XXXX), status tracking, document upload,
   live SSE updates, email + in-app notifications.
5. **NORIA everywhere:** the bar on the homepage, the elevated ✦ button on mobile, the
   Platforms dropdown, the widget on subpages. Never hangs: dedicated engine (8s clock) →
   platform AI in NORIA's persona (20s) → built-in FAQ floor. Voice in/out supported.
6. **Wizards:** every multi-step flow has Next/Back, progress indication, and full-width
   primary buttons near the bottom on mobile.

# PART F — PLATFORM & QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

- **PWA:** installable, service-worker versioned so every deployment reaches every installed
  app automatically; network-first for code, cache-first for media.
- **Mobile-native:** single column, bottom nav, 48px targets, safe-area insets, zero
  horizontal overflow, tight hero padding.
- **Accessibility:** contrast per the scales above (hero headlines pure black on white),
  focus states, screen-reader labels, keyboard paths, color never the only signal.
- **Performance:** system-font fallbacks, lazy media, no blocking third parties beyond fonts.
- **Testing bar:** every feature browser-tested (desktop + phone viewports) and flow-tested
  end-to-end before ship; zero JS errors tolerated.
- **Sample data:** never use real nationalities/names of the people we serve as dummy data.

## NEVER DO
Long dark scrolling public backgrounds · gold overuse · orange outside NORIA/live-activity ·
mixed icon families · white text on white/gold · fake statistics · crowded navs · heavy
shadows · decorative animation · mailto links or app-choosers for contact · silent failures ·
a travel-agency first impression.
