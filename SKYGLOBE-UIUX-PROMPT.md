# SKYGLOBE GROUP — UI/UX MASTER PROMPT
### The complete, self-contained design brief for any designer, developer, or AI asked to create or evaluate a SkyGlobe Group interface.

---

## THE ASSIGNMENT

Design the user interface and experience for **SKYGLOBE GROUP** — a global digital ecosystem
headquartered in Africa, positioned among the defining platforms of the century. It is not a
travel website. It is the gateway to a family of platforms:

- **SKYGLOBE GROUP** — the parent: global innovation and infrastructure.
- **NORIA ✦** — the intelligence layer: AI embedded in every service (not a chatbot product).
- **TERRA** — Africa's trust infrastructure: identity, verification, records, security.
- **YUNEX** — Africa's economic infrastructure: payments, commerce, cross-border trade.
- **Service verticals** — Global Mobility (visas, permits, travel), SkyGlobe Academy
  (education for every age), Legal & Trust Services, Digital Identity, Finance & Payments,
  Innovation & Technology.

The emotional target on first impression: *“This is a serious global technology organization —
the trust of a financial institution, the innovation of a technology company, the elegance of
a luxury brand, the accessibility of a consumer platform.”* The user should feel they are
**entering an ecosystem, not visiting a website.**

---

## 1. FOUNDATION: LIGHT-FIRST, SOVEREIGN, LUMINOUS

**The canvas is light.** White and soft-white backgrounds dominate (~60% of every screen).
Dark surfaces are deliberate, rare, and always *short* — never long scrolling walls of dark.

| Surface | Background |
|---|---|
| Hero | Luminous white with faint blue tint (`#eef4ff → #f8fafc` radial) and subtle gold/blue glows |
| Content & forms | White `#ffffff` on soft white `#F8FAFC` |
| Feature strips | Light gray `#eef2f8` |
| CTA bands | Deep Royal Blue `#0A2E65 → #15418c` — short bands only |
| Footer | Midnight Navy `#041022` — the one standing dark surface |
| **Command centers (CEO/staff portals)** | Deep midnight — dashboards are the exception where dark rules, so gold data glows and eyes rest |

## 2. COLOR LAW

| Color | Hex | Role | Discipline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Deep Royal Blue | `#0A2E65` | Brand structure, navigation, trust, headings accents | ~25% of the screen |
| Premium Gold | `#D4A73A` (deep `#a87016` for text on light) | Primary CTAs, highlights, key metrics | ~10%, scarce by law — scarcity is its power |
| Vibrant Orange | `#e65100 → #ff9f1c` | **Reserved: the NORIA ✦ star + live activity only.** Never for errors. | ~5% |
| Charcoal | `#111827 / #041022` | Primary text on light | — |
| Grays | `#4B5563` secondary · `#6B7280` supporting | Text scale on light | — |
| On dark | `#ffffff` primary · `#D1D5DB` secondary · `#9CA3AF` muted | Text scale on dark | — |

**Platform identities (constitutional — they follow the real commissioned logos):**
NORIA = orange ✦ + gold · TERRA = green `#3fae6a/#2c7d4c` + deep blue · YUNEX = blue→purple
gradient `#3d5af1 → #b326d9` + gold. Never reassign these; never give YUNEX orange (it
collides with NORIA's sacred mark).

**The NORIA star ✦** appears at the upper-right of every SkyGlobe logo — small, orange,
permanent. It declares: intelligence lives inside everything we build.

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY
**Inter**, one family everywhere (system fallback). Georgia serif is permitted solely for
display/manifesto headlines. Hierarchy: Display → Heading → Subheading → Body → Caption.
Large headlines. Short sentences. Confident, imperative microcopy: “Continue”, “Verify
Identity”, “Explore Ecosystem” — never “Click here to…”.

## 4. LAYOUT, SHAPE, ELEVATION
8-point spacing grid (8/16/24/32/48/64/80/96). Radii: buttons/inputs 12px, cards 14–16px,
dialogs 18–20px. Shadows soft and layered (`0 6px 20px rgba(4,16,34,.06)`); never heavy.
Generous whitespace; minimal clutter; one primary action per card.

## 5. SIGNATURE COMPONENTS
- **Premium card:** white, soft radius, hairline border, gentle lift on hover, colored
  *top border* in the owning platform's accent (orange/green/blue-purple) to mark territory.
- **Kicker chip:** small uppercase letter-spaced label in deep gold on a `#fdf6e3` pill —
  crowns every major section (“THE ECOSYSTEM”, “SOLUTIONS”).
- **Buttons:** primary gold-filled (dark text), secondary blue-filled (white text),
  tertiary outlined white (charcoal text), text-only for minor actions. 48px touch targets.
- **Manifesto block:** white card, 4px platform-accent left border, Georgia serif prose.

## 6. NAVIGATION
**Desktop top bar (white glass):** Logo✦ · Home · **Platforms ✦** (a featured dropdown
presenting NORIA / TERRA / YUNEX each with icon, name, identity line — the platforms hold
court at the top of the site with authority) · Global Mobility ▾ (ALL travel: visas, permits,
recruitment, flights, hotels, insurance, packages, events, destinations) · Academy ▾ ·
Solutions ▾ · Company ▾ · Login · gold CTA.
**Mobile:** fixed white bottom bar — Home · Discover · **NORIA ✦ elevated gold circle at
center** · Services · Profile — plus a full-screen menu whose first section is Platforms.
**Every page** carries Back ‹ › Next floating navigation. Users always know how to move.

## 7. THE HOMEPAGE, SECTION BY SECTION
1. **Hero (light):** kicker chip “SKYGLOBE GROUP · A DIGITAL ECOSYSTEM ✦” → headline
   “Building the **Intelligent Foundation** of Tomorrow.” (gradient gold→blue on the key
   phrase) → one-sentence mission → search → [Explore Ecosystem →] gold + [Partner With Us]
   outline → honest stat strip (real numbers only) → “One World. One Mission. ✦”.
2. **The Ecosystem (soft-white):** “One Group. One Mission. Many Platforms.” — three platform
   cards (NORIA/TERRA/YUNEX, accent top-borders, identity lines, “Terra verifies. YUNEX
   moves. NORIA thinks.”) above a row of vertical mini-cards (Travel first among equals).
3. **Solutions for Every World (light gray):** four audience doors — Individuals, Businesses,
   Governments & Institutions, Developers — real links only, honest about roadmap items.
4. **Principles band (short royal-blue):** Security · Transparency · Innovation · Human Progress.
5. Service showcases, proof, insights — light.
6. **Footer (midnight):** Ecosystem column first (NORIA, TERRA, YUNEX, the verticals),
   then Services, Company, Contact.

## 8. MOTION
Fade, slide, gentle scale, soft glow. Cards rise into place; nothing appears instantly;
nothing loops for decoration. `prefers-reduced-motion` always honored.

## 9. ACCESSIBILITY (non-negotiable)
Contrast per the text scales above · 48px touch targets · visible focus states ·
screen-reader labels · keyboard paths · color never the only carrier of meaning
(icon + word always accompany it).

## 10. NATIVE PER DEVICE
Phone: single column, bottom nav, thumb-first, no horizontal overflow, safe-area aware.
Tablet: two columns. Desktop: workspace with rich sections. The layout adapts;
the language never changes.

## 11. VOICE
Confident, warm, precise. Claims-honest: never “first in the world”; always “a new
architecture uniquely designed for Africa's needs — African-owned, African-governed.”
Signature lines: *“One World. One Mission.”* · *“Terra verifies. YUNEX moves. NORIA thinks.”*
· *“No verification, no trade.”* (YUNEX Trade).

## 12. WHAT TO NEVER DO
Long dark scrolling backgrounds on public pages · gold overuse · orange for anything but
NORIA/live-activity · mixed icon families · white text on white/gold surfaces · fake
statistics · crowded navs · heavy shadows · animation for its own sake · a travel-agency
first impression.
