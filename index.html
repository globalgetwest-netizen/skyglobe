Earn a Real Certificate

A certificate is only accepted by institutions, employers and embassies when it is genuinely earned and verifiable. Here is how to get one the right way — no fakes, no risk of a visa ban.

⚠️ Important: SkyGlobe never sells or fakes work-experience or academic certificates. A forged certificate is easily verified and can lead to a permanent visa ban or job termination. The routes below give you a genuine, verifiable certificate accepted anywhere.
📜

Work Experience Certificate

From your real employer

Use our Experience Certificate generator above to create a polished draft, then ask your current/former employer's HR or manager to issue it on the company's official letterhead with a signature & stamp. That makes it legally valid and verifiable.

🎓

Accredited Online Courses

Globally recognised

Genuinely earn a certificate from accredited platforms — Coursera, edX, Google Career Certificates, Microsoft Learn and LinkedIn Learning. Many are free to study (you pay only for the certificate) and are recognised by employers worldwide.

🏅

Professional Bodies

Industry-standard

For regulated fields, earn certification from the official body — e.g. PMP (project management), ACCA/CPA (finance), CompTIA/AWS/Cisco (IT), IELTS/TOEFL (English). These are the gold standard for visas and skilled migration.
