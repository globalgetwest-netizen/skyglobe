📡 Official Communication Hierarchy
All internal communication at SkyGlobe Group follows this chain. Staff raise issues to their Director first. Directors escalate to the CEO when required. Direct CEO contact is reserved for urgent or policy-level matters.
1
Staff Member raises a matter Case Officer, Specialist, Clerk, or any front-line team member identifies an issue, update or request.
2
Reports to Department Director Staff member contacts their direct Department Director via Team Chat, WhatsApp or internal message. Director acknowledges within the same working day.
3
Director reviews and acts (or escalates) Director resolves operational matters independently. Policy decisions, financial approvals above threshold, or legal matters are escalated to the CEO with a written summary.
4
CEO makes final decision CEO reviews escalations, approves or instructs. Decision is communicated back down the chain: CEO → Director → Staff Member.
5
Action is confirmed and logged The outcome is noted in the application or staff record. Client is notified by the assigned Client Relations Officer if the matter affects a client file.
Staff → Director
Internal Issues & Work Updates
Use Team Chat or WhatsApp for quick matters. For formal requests (leave, resources, client complaints), message your Director directly.
Director → CEO
Escalations & Approvals
Directors contact CEO for: new service pricing, refund approvals, contract sign-offs, legal matters, and any situation outside department authority.
CEO → All
Policy & Strategic Directives
CEO issues directives via Team Chat or direct message to Directors. Directors cascade instructions to their department staff within 2 hours.
Urgent / Emergency
Direct CEO WhatsApp
For genuine emergencies (client legal issue, data breach, urgent deadline), any staff member may contact CEO directly via WhatsApp — state "URGENT" in first line.
