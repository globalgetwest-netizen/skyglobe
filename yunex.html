🛡️ TRUST BY DESIGN — THE YUNEX TRADE RULE

Every person on YUNEX Trade is a real, verified human or business. Individuals register with their national identity (real KYC, verified through TERRA). Businesses — and every partner of a business — register with their government-issued business registration and the legal identity of each owner and partner, from their own country. Buyers and sellers across China, the Gulf, Europe, the Americas, Asia and all of Africa showcase products, list, negotiate and transact in real time, with real people — end-to-end encrypted, with zero tolerance for illegal activity: the system detects, blocks and reports it, under full platform control. No verification, no trade.

How it will work: a Ghanaian business owner finds a verified supplier through YUNEX Trade · TERRA verifies the supplier · YUNEX handles payment · NORIA ✦ negotiates, translates and analyzes · logistics partners deliver.

The Manifesto

A continent of traders was carved into markets too small to prosper alone. Borders drawn in distant rooms still tax a farmer's harvest and a young builder's dream. Money crosses oceans more easily than it crosses the river to the next country.

YUNEX exists to end this. We are building the economic engine of Africa — where payment is instant, trade is trusted, credit is earned by real work, and a young person's enterprise can reach every corner of the continent from day one.

YUNEX runs on trust — the trust TERRA builds. Together they are one ecosystem: Terra verifies, YUNEX moves. The wealth Africa creates will circulate in Africa, build in Africa, and belong to Africa.
